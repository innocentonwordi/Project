NIGERIAN FRIED RICE

Nigerian Fried Rice is a vibrant party staple made by stir-frying parboiled rice
with mixed vegetables, liver or shrimp, and curry-spiced stock. It’s colorful,
slightly sweet from the veggies, and packed with savory flavor

Dish Preview

Ingredients

  • 2 Cup Cooked Rice
  • Chicken Stock
  • Tablespoon Oil For Frying
  • Onions (Diced)
  • Mixed Vegetables (Carrot, Green Beans, Sweet Corn)
  • Thyme
  • Curry Powder
  • Chicken Stock Cube
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Liver

Instructions

  1. Add the boiled rice to the stock in a medium pot and cook on medium
    heat until the water is dried up.
  2. Preheat the oil in a pan on medium to high heat, then put in the onions,
    and fry for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables, thyme, curry, salt and stock cubes.
  4. Put in the liver and rice, then stir-fry for about 3-5 minutes
    on high heat
  5. Take it off the heat and serve.
